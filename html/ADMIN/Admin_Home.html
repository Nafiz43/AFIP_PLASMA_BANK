Message From CAS

Genaral Aziz Ahmed, SBP, BSP, BGBM, PBGBM, BGBMS, psc, G

I am optimistic about Plasma therapy by Combined Military Hospital(CMH), as part of a clinical trial to help fighting against the COVID-19. The plasma bank app developed jointly by Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST) and CMH will make an effective bridge between plasma donors and patients.


About the web app

CMH plasma bank app is a unique app which works as a database for storing details about donor and stored plasma information. The main purpose of the app is to create and manage a platform for plasma donors in CMH. This app will help the patients in CMH to get required plasma fast. It will also help the doctors to check the availability of plasma in the plasma bank.

About us

We, a team of students and faculties of CSE Department of MIST under the direct supervision of Commandant, MIST in collaboration with CMH prepared a plasma bank app to make and manage a platform for plasma donors and accumulated plasma in CMH.
